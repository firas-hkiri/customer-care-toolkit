Customer Care Toolkit

A modern, fast, and fully interactive Customer Care Toolkit designed and built by Firas Hkiri to help support agents work faster, respond professionally, and stay consistent across all customer interactions.

This toolkit centralizes:

Ready-to-use chat templates

Complete email templates

Replacement & refund scripts

Shipping & tracking replies

Design & technical troubleshooting

Pre-order probing questions

Operations & account support

Case notes generator

And a smart global search engine that instantly filters topics, subcategories, and lines.

Its goal:
Save time, increase accuracy, and provide human-friendly customer care.
